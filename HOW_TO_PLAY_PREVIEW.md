## 🎨 Color Scheme

### Text Colors
- **Headings**: Black (text-foreground)
- **Body Text**: Black (text-foreground)
- **Secondary Text**: Gray (text-muted-foreground)
- **Accent Text**: Pink (text-secondary) and Green (text-accent)

### Background Colors
- **Page Background**: White (bg-background)
- **Card Backgrounds**: Light gray (bg-card)
- **Inner Cards**: White (bg-background)

### Borders & Shadows
- **Borders**: Thick black brutal borders
- **Shadows**: Pink and lime drop shadows
- **Hover Effects**: Translate + shadow removal

---

## 📐 Layout Structure

### Spacing
- **Section Spacing**: 6-8 units (space-y-6, space-y-8)
- **Card Padding**: 6-8 units (p-6, p-8)
- **Grid Gaps**: 2-4 units (gap-2, gap-4)

### Typography
- **Page Title**: text-4xl to text-7xl
- **Section Headings**: text-2xl to text-4xl
- **Body Text**: text-sm to text-xl
- **Small Text**: text-xs to text-sm

### Responsive Adjustments
- **Mobile**: Smaller text, reduced padding
- **Desktop**: Larger text, increased spacing
- **Grid**: 2 columns mobile, 3 columns desktop

---

## 🎯 Key Features

### Visual Hierarchy
1. **Page Title** - Largest, most prominent
2. **Section Headings** - Bold, with emojis
3. **Step Labels** - Medium bold
4. **Body Text** - Regular weight
5. **Tips** - Smaller, with icons

### Interactive Elements
- **BACK TO HOME Button**: Large, green, hover effect
- **All Text**: Readable, high contrast
- **Cards**: Clickable appearance with shadows

### Accessibility
- **High Contrast**: Black text on white backgrounds
- **Large Touch Targets**: Buttons are large and easy to tap
- **Clear Hierarchy**: Headings and sections are well-defined
- **Readable Fonts**: Bold, sans-serif typography
