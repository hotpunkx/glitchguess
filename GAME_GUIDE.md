# GLITCHGUESS - Game Guide

## 🎮 What is GLITCHGUESS?

GLITCHGUESS is an interactive 20 questions guessing game featuring AI-powered gameplay. Challenge the AI or let the AI challenge you in this exciting battle of wits!

## 🎯 Game Modes

### Mode 1: I Think of Something – AI Guesses
1. Think of any object, person, animal, movie, or place (keep it secret!)
2. The AI will ask you up to 20 yes/no questions
3. Answer with "Yes", "No", or "Sometimes"
4. The AI will try to guess what you're thinking of
5. Can the AI figure it out before 20 questions?

### Mode 2: AI Thinks of Something – I Guess
1. The AI secretly picks something
2. You ask yes/no questions (up to 20)
3. The AI responds with "Yes", "No", or "Sometimes"
4. Make your guess at any time!
5. Can you figure out what the AI is thinking?

## 🎨 Design Features

- **Neubrutalism Style**: Bold, high-contrast design with thick borders and dramatic shadows
- **Color Scheme**: Pure black & white with hot pink and electric lime accents
- **Responsive**: Optimized for both desktop and mobile devices
- **Animations**: Victory confetti and smooth transitions

## 🚀 How to Play

1. **Start**: Choose your game mode from the start screen
2. **Play**: Answer or ask questions strategically
3. **Track Progress**: Watch the question counter and review history
4. **Win or Lose**: See the dramatic reveal at the end
5. **Share**: Share your results with friends!

## 💡 Tips for Success

- **Be Strategic**: Ask broad questions first, then narrow down
- **Use "Sometimes"**: When the answer isn't clear-cut
- **Think Creatively**: The AI can think of unusual things!
- **Review History**: Check previous Q&A to avoid repeating questions

## 🎉 Victory Conditions

- **Win**: Guess correctly before 20 questions
- **Lose**: Reach 20 questions without guessing correctly
- **Celebrate**: Enjoy the confetti animation on victory!
